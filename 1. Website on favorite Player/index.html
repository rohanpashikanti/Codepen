Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Captain Wicketkeeper Finisher

MS Dhoni, a legendary Indian cricketer, rose from wicket-keeping in local matches to become a powerful batsman and captain. Debuting in 2004, he led India to victory in the 2007 T20 World Cup, the 2011 World Cup, and the 2013 Champions Trophy. Known for his cool finishing and iconic helicopter shot, Dhoni is one of India's most decorated captains.
